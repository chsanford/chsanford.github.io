Spliceman

As a Hassenfeld Child Health Innovation Institute Summer Scholar for summer 2016, I worked with Professor William Fairbrother's lab on Spliceman, which which is software that assesses the likelihood of a mutation affecting RNA splcing. I worked on the front end and back end of the web app. I used MySQL to store previously processed mutations in a database.

Fractals

After taking Topics in Chaotic Dynamics (APMA 1360) in spring 2016, I was particularly interested in fractals, both theoretically and visually. I have learned more about fractal dimension since taking the class by reading Fractal Geometry by Kenneth Falconer. I have worked on a web app that draws various types of fractals as an aid for visualization.

Water Scarcity

I competed in the 2016 Interdisciplinary Contest in Modeling with two teammates. We modeled water scarcity in India and proposed solutions to the long term risks. We compiled our findings in a paper. We were selected as outstanding winners, being one of the top five teams out of three thousand. Our paper will be published in the Undergraduate Mathematics and Its Applications journal.

Water Scarcity

I competed in the 2016 Interdisciplinary Contest in Modeling with two teammates. We modeled water scarcity in India and proposed solutions to the long term risks. We compiled our findings in a paper. We were selected as outstanding winners, being one of the top five teams out of three thousand. Our paper will be published in the Undergraduate Mathematics and Its Applications journal.

Columbia CS Theory Student Reading Group

Starting fall 2019, this group is a setting for Columbia PhD students and postdocs to read and discuss academic papers about theoretical computer science. We meet at 5pm in CSB 488 and communicate primarily on the theory-student-seminar of the Columbia CS PhD Students Slack. Please contact Clayton if you have any questions about the seminar.
